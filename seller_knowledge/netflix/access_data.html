How to request your data from Netfix and upload to OfUser

Author: OfUser | Date：4/9/2025

In this article, we would like to walk you through how to request your Netflix Data and upload it to OfUser

Download Data from Netflix

1. Go to Account

2. On the left-hand side, click on Security

3. Click on Personal info access

4. Clicking on ‘Confirm Request’ will take you to the main page, please enter your password to confirm the request.

5. Once you click on the above button, you should receive an email from Netflix, asking you to confirm the request.

6. After 1-3 days, you should receive an email with a subject line “Your download is ready”

7. Click Download

Work with OfUser

1. Once you received .zip file from Netflix, download the file and unzip on your local file. It would contain all of your requested data from Netflix

2. Find out the campaign you're interested in Start Now
