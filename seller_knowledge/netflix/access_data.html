How to request your data from Netfix and upload to OfUser

Author: OfUser | Date：4/9/2025

In this article, we would like to walk you through how to request your Netflix Data and upload it to OfUser

Download Data from Netflix

1. Click on your profile, then click on “Viewing Activity” in the Preferences section

2. Scroll through the bottom of the page, then find “Download all”

3. After you click on Download all, your viewing activity data should be saved in your folder (this data only contains your viewing titles and viewing date, and does not include any of your personal information)

4. You can now upload the data to our platform. Thank you!
