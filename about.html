OfUser Story

With over 16 years of combined experience in the tech industry, we founded OfUser to build a more transparent and empowering data ecosystem. Our mission is simple: to give individuals the tools to understand, control, and earn from their data—while helping companies unlock responsible and ethical monetization opportunities.

Having worked at leading tech companies like Google, Amazon, Airbnb, and Instacart, we've seen firsthand the immense value user data can provide. But we also saw a disconnect: most people aren't aware of their rights when it comes to their own data—let alone how to benefit from it. At the same time, companies often face challenges navigating complex data regulations, sometimes resulting in costly fines and reputational damage.

At OfUser, we believe it doesn’t have to be this way. We're creating a two-sided data marketplace designed to bring clarity, trust, and mutual value to both users and businesses. By putting transparency and user empowerment at the core, we’re building a future where data works for everyone.
